What is HANOK?

Hanok refers to houses built in the traditional Korean style.
While tile-roofed and thatch-roofed hanoks were equally common,
the former were typically noblemen residences while the latter were mostly houses of the commoners in the past.
These days, most traditional hanok that are still used for housing have modern facilities installed within.
